<strong>Challenges</strong></p><p>
</p><p><strong>Competition from Alternative Coatings</strong>: While self-cleaning glass is an innovative solution, it faces competition from other types of coatings, such as hydrophobic and hydrophilic coatings, which can also offer some degree of self-cleaning capabilities but may be less expensive.</p><p><strong>Environmental Factors</strong>: The effectiveness of self-cleaning glass is influenced by environmental factors like weather conditions, air quality, and pollution levels.
