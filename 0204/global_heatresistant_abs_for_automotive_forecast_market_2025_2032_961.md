<h1>Heat-resistant ABS for Automotive Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Heat-resistant ABS (HH ABS) is a specialized grade of Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) engineered to withstand higher temperatures without compromising its mechanical properties. Known for its superior heat resistance, shock absorption, and excellent workability, HH ABS is widely utilized in the automotive industry. It is distinguished by its high strength and moderate ductility, making it ideal for components that require durability under thermal stress. Key applications include automotive interiors, exterior parts, and under-the-hood components where consistent performance under heat is essential.</p><p>
